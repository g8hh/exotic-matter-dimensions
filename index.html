Exotic Matter

At the start of the game, wait for your exotic matter to go up and then buy axis. There isn't much more to the game at this stage.

Tributes

When you reach undefined exotic matter, you will unlock the option to reset your progress in exchange for Tributes. Tributes give you boosts depending on how many you have, and they can also be spent on upgrades.

However, tributes are a static prestige layer, meaning that the more you have, the more exotic matter you need to reach to get more.

Enhancers

Enhancers are a feature where you can buy upgrades with Enhancers, which increase in cost when you buy one.

Depending on how many Enhancers you have, you can only pick Enhancers from one row, not the whole board.

Dark Matter

Dark Matter is a new type of matter. It is generated by dark axis instead of normal axis, and for every 4 dark axis bought you get a normal axis.

If you get enough dark axis, you can reset your dark matter to gain an Axiom, which boosts dark matter and a specific dark axis.

Energy

In Energy there are six types of energy, each boosting a different resource. Energy increases exponentially until it reaches its respective resource, at what point it begins increasing slower but the resource is boosted.
